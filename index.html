How It Works

Upload your PDF files through our secure form, and we'll take care of the rest. Your file is received by our verified printing partners who will print and deliver it to your doorstep. Simple, fast, and affordable printing service made for students and professionals.

About Us

PrintXdrop is a student-led initiative to make printing documents easy and accessible. We connect users who need documents printed with local shop owners who can print and deliver quickly. We believe in simplicity, affordability, and speed.
